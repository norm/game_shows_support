<p>
  Palazzo Comunale, San Gimignano’s town hall, was built as the seat of the
  municipal government in 1288.
</p>

<p>
  As befits the City of Towers, one of the council’s earliest acts inside
  their new home was to construct the Torre Grossa on top. The Podestà took up
  residence at the Palazzo Comunale in 1337.
</p>
