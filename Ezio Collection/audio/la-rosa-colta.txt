<p>
  Opened in 1465, La Rosa Colta (The Plucked Rose), quickly became essential
  to Florentine life. The women there were known for being upper-class, they
  were educated in poetry and the arts, as well as sexual intercourse.
</p>

<p>
  It was not rare to see members of the signoria at La Rosa Colta, and even on
  occasion, a Medici.
</p>
